How long is Metroid Prime 4: Beyond?
When focusing on the main objectives, Metroid Prime 4: Beyond is about 11½ Hours in length. If you're a gamer that strives to see all aspects of the game, you are likely to spend around 15½ Hours to obtain 100% completion.
